What is PhysicsAssist?

Knowing us

.

PhysicsAssist is your next best assistant for your physics need. It may look sophisticated at first, but it will help. PhysicsAssist allows you to solve complex equations and shows you a comprehensive way to solve them. Our main objective is to help those who take science as their main course and SPM students. We noticed that it requires a substantial amount of time to solve a complex equation as there are considerable numbers to put in their calculator. That is where the idea came in. We want to build a tool to ease them. On that note, we sincerely hope that students who use PhysicsAssist can improve their physics plus their grades. We encourage students to try and understand the equation to ensure their probability of solving the questions on their own.
